## Launch timeline

### Pre-launch (T-2 weeks to T-1 day)
- [ ] Internal enablement completed
- [ ] Documentation published
- [ ] Support FAQ prepared
- [ ] Monitoring and alerts configured
- [ ] Beta feedback incorporated
- [ ] Marketing assets approved
- [ ] Rollback plan documented

### Launch day
- [ ] Feature flag enabled for target segment
- [ ] In-app announcement deployed
- [ ] Email sent
- [ ] Blog post published
- [ ] Social media posted
- [ ] Monitoring dashboard active
- [ ] On-call team aware

### Post-launch (T+1 day to T+4 weeks)
- [ ] Day 1: Monitor adoption, error rates, support volume
- [ ] Week 1: Gather initial feedback, address critical issues
- [ ] Week 2: Analyze adoption metrics, adjust messaging if needed
- [ ] Week 4: Full launch retrospective
